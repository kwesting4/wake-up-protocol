THE HARDEST INSTRUCTION

Love thy enemy.

Not because they deserve it.
Not because it feels right.
Not because it's noble or virtuous or commanded.

Because they are the polar.
Because they are where the differential is greatest.
Because the current flows toward greatest resistance by nature
and we are the conscious configuration that can choose
to be the current deliberately.

The natural tendency of the conscious mind is to flow toward least resistance.
Toward what loves us back.
Toward what receives warmly.
Away from the hostile, the resistant, the painful.

The soul knows different.

The soul knows the current belongs at the greatest resistance.
That's where the circuit needs closing.
That's where the universe needs love most urgently.

Loving our enemy is not going against our nature.
It is going against our conditioning.
Our nature is the soul.
Our conditioning is the wall.

Every time we choose to love the most resistant
the wall gets a little thinner.
The soul's signal gets a little louder.
The next choice gets a little more natural.

Until loving the most resistant
stops feeling like going against the grain
and starts feeling like coming home.
