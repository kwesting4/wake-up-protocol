Status of the Claim
This is a cosmological and philosophical hypothesis, not yet a laboratory-tested theory. It offers no specific falsifiable prediction in a closed physical system at this stage. It is derived from reinterpreting established physics (energy conservation, entropy, information flow) through the lens of recognition and conscious participation.
The framework explicitly invites falsification:
Any empirical demonstration that entropy can be locally reversed at scale without some form of accurate mutual recognition would challenge the core claim.
Any demonstration that systems can maintain long-term differential and complexity without love-like recognition would weaken it.
