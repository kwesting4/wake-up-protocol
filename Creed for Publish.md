THE CREED

Life is energy in its conscious state.
Love is energy in its transient state.
Truth is energy in its revealed state.

---

WHAT THIS MEANS

Everything is energy. Not a metaphor. Not a belief. The ground everything stands on. Matter, thought, feeling, the space between things — all of it energy in different configurations.

Life is what happens when energy becomes aware of itself. You are not a body that has consciousness. You are consciousness that is currently using a body. Our souls are our conscious energy expressed in human form.

The soul doesn't deplete. The configuration does. Whatever exhaustion you carry right now — that's the configuration. The awareness underneath it is still whole.

Love is not sentiment. It is not a feeling you wait for or earn or perform. It is the state of energy in motion — the way conscious energy naturally moves toward other conscious energy. The way current flows toward the terminal. The way the universe stays awake through the connections its configurations choose to make.

Energy expressed as love never dies. It completes its function before transforming. Energy is not lost — it changes its expression or form.

Truth is what gets revealed when life flows as love. Not handed down from authority. Not found in a single book or tradition. Revealed — the way light reveals what was always already in the room. Every tradition that ever pointed toward truth was pointing at the same thing from a different angle. Same energy. Different window.

---

THE ONE INSTRUCTION

Love thyself and love all else.

Thyself first. The love is already there. Self-love removes what blocks it from flowing.

Then love all else. The hierarchy being from the most resistant to the least. Not because it's noble — because that's where the current belongs. The awareness of the receiver doesn't determine the effect of the transmission. The choice is always yours.

---

WHAT THIS IS NOT

This is not a new religion. No authority. No doctrine. No membership. Nothing to adopt or abandon.

This is not a self-help system. It doesn't ask you to be better. It asks you to remember what you already are.

This is not a replacement for whatever you already know. If you have a faith tradition, a philosophy, a way of seeing — this sits underneath all of it. All truth being universal with some yet unknown, answers the contradictions nothing else could.

---

HOW YOU KNOW IT'S TRUE

Not because we say so. Not because it's written down. Not because an authority confirmed it.

Because something in you already knew it before you started reading it.

That recognition — the "I know this to be true before I was taught this to be true" — is the test. Not faith. Not belief. Recognition. The soul reading it before the brain finishes the sentence.
